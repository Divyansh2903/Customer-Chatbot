Acme Supply Co. — Shipping & Delivery

Order processing
Orders placed before 1:00 PM Pacific Time on a business day are processed and
handed to the carrier the same day. Orders placed after the cutoff, on weekends,
or on US public holidays are processed the next business day.

Shipping methods and cost
- Standard (3–5 business days): free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99.
- Expedited (2 business days): $14.99.
- Overnight (next business day): $29.99.
Shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on the destination and the method
selected. We currently ship to the United States and Canada only.

Tracking
A tracking number is emailed to you as soon as your order ships. You can also
find it under Account → Orders. Tracking information can take up to 24 hours to
appear on the carrier's site after the label is created.

Lost or delayed packages
If your tracking has not updated in 5 business days, or the carrier marks a
package as delivered but you have not received it, contact support within 14 days
of the ship date and we will open a claim and send a replacement.
