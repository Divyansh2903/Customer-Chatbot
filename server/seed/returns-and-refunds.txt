Acme Supply Co. — Returns & Refunds

Return window
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items
must be unused, in their original packaging, and include all accessories.

Non-returnable items
Gift cards, downloadable software, and clearance items marked "Final Sale"
cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to start a return
Go to Account → Orders, select the order, and choose "Return item." We email you
a prepaid return label. Drop the package at any carrier location within 14 days
of receiving the label.

Refund timing
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are issued to the original
payment method within 5–7 business days. The original shipping charge is refunded
only if the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item).

Exchanges
We do not process direct exchanges. To swap an item, return the original for a
refund and place a new order.

Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within 48 hours with a
photo. We cover return shipping and send a replacement at no cost.
