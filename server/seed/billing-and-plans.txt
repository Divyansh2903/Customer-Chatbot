Acme Supply Co. — Billing & Membership Plans

Membership tiers
- Free: standard pricing, no annual fee.
- Plus ($49/year): free standard shipping on every order and early access to sales.
- Pro ($99/year): everything in Plus, plus free expedited shipping and a dedicated
  support line.

Billing cycle
Paid memberships are billed once per year on the date you subscribe and renew
automatically. We email a reminder 7 days before each renewal.

Payment methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and PayPal. We do not
accept checks, wire transfers, or cryptocurrency.

Updating payment details
Update your card under Account → Billing. Changes apply to your next charge.

Canceling a membership
You can cancel anytime under Account → Billing → Cancel membership. Your benefits
remain active until the end of the current paid year; we do not prorate refunds
for the unused portion.

Invoices and receipts
A receipt is emailed after every charge. Full invoices are available under
Account → Billing → History and can be downloaded as PDF.
